>[!property] Property (Additivity for Independent Distributions).
>Suppose that $p_{1},\ q_{1}\in\Delta(\mathcal{X}_{1})$ and $p_{2},\ q_{2}\in\Delta(\mathcal{X}_{2})$ are independent distributions with,
>$$
> p(x_{1},x_{2})=p_{1}(x_{1})p_{2}(x_{2}),\qquad q(x_{1},x_{2})=q_{1}(x_{1})q_{2}(x_{2})
> $$
>Then, 
>$$
>D(p\mid  \mid  q)=D(p_{1}\mid  \mid  q_{1})+D(p_{2}\mid  \mid  q_{2})
>$$
