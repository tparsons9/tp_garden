>[!property] Property. (Probability-Zero States)
>
>$$
>H(p)=H(p_{1},\dots,p_{n},0),\qquad \forall\,n<\infty,\  p \in \Delta(\{ \theta_{1},\dots,\theta_{n} \})
>$$

Entropy is unchanged by an expansion of the state space to include probability-zero outcomes.
