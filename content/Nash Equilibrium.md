**Nash Equilibrium** (**NE**) is the _main_ solution concept for [[Strategic Games]].

Specifically, it is a [[Strategy Profile]] with the property that,

> _No player can get higher payoff by choosing a strategy different from the one prescribed by the profile, given that all other players adhere to the strategies prescribed by the profile._

A strategy profile $s ^{\ast}=(s_{i}^{\ast})^{N}_{i=1}\in S$ is a **Nash Equilibrium** if for every player $i$ and for each strategy $s_{i}\in S_{i}$, $s_{i}^{\ast}\in BR_{i}(s_{-i}^{\ast})$, or it is a [[Best Response]].

> [!rocket] Intuition
> No player can gain more by changing their own strategy (holding all other players strategies fixed) in a game.
>
> There is no possibility of increasing their respective [[Expected payoff of a strategy given a belief]] , while the other player's keep theirs unchanged.
